SECTION 2 — THE PROBLEM ONESELECT SOLVES
================================================================================

Hiring is broken for most companies, and OneSelect was built specifically to
fix the problems that plague the traditional process.

THE TIME PROBLEM
Traditional recruitment takes 4–6 weeks minimum from job posting to shortlist.
HR teams spend an average of 23+ hours screening CVs per open role. The average
job posting receives 250 CVs. Reading each one for even 5 minutes means 20+
hours of reading before a single interview has been scheduled. First-round
interviews then take additional days or weeks to coordinate, with multiple
back-and-forth emails and calendar clashes.

THE COST PROBLEM
Traditional recruitment agencies charge 15–25% of the candidate's first-year
salary as a placement fee. For a role paying £50,000 per year, that means
£7,500–£12,500 per hire. For a company hiring 5–10 people per year, this adds
up to £37,500–£125,000 annually — just in agency fees, before any other
recruitment costs. Small and medium businesses often cannot afford this model,
leaving them under-resourced or forced to do everything internally.

THE CONSISTENCY AND BIAS PROBLEM
Human CV screening is inconsistent. The same CV reviewed on a Monday morning
versus a Friday afternoon may receive different attention. Unconscious bias
affects decisions based on name, school, job title formatting, or which page
a CV landed on. Good candidates get missed because their CV was buried on page
12 of 250. Weaker candidates make it through because they happened to catch a
reviewer at the right moment. There is no systematic, repeatable scoring.

THE SCHEDULING PROBLEM
Even after screening, arranging first-round interviews with 10–20 candidates
across multiple calendars adds another week of administrative overhead. For
every candidate who cancels or no-shows, more time is lost.

THE SMALL TEAM PROBLEM
Companies with small HR teams — or no dedicated HR function at all — lack the
bandwidth to run a proper recruitment process for multiple simultaneous roles.
They either hire badly, hire slowly, or pay agency fees they can't really afford.

ONESELECT'S SOLUTION
OneSelect eliminates all of these problems. The AI processes 100 CVs in under
10 minutes. Every candidate is evaluated identically, with no human bias.
Interviews happen automatically with no scheduling overhead. Clients receive a
ranked shortlist the same day. The total cost is a flat fee — not a percentage
of salary — saving 60–80% vs a traditional agency.

SECTION 3 — HOW ONESELECT WORKS — COMPLETE WORKFLOW
================================================================================

OneSelect runs a structured, automated pipeline that takes candidates from
raw CV to ranked, interviewed shortlist without the client needing to be
involved in any of the heavy lifting. Here is exactly how it works.

---

STEP 1 — CLIENT ONBOARDING

When a company engages OneSelect, they are set up with a private client portal.
The client receives a branded welcome email with login credentials for their
personal portal, hosted at oneselect-ai-t6uo-phi.vercel.app. On first login,
they are prompted to set a secure password. Their dashboard immediately shows
all their active roles, pipeline status, and any candidates already in the
system. The onboarding process takes less than 24 hours.

---

STEP 2 — JOB DEFINITION

The client creates their job requirements through the portal's job wizard.
They fill in:
- Job title
- Years of experience required
- Required skills (must-have, non-negotiable)
- Preferred skills (nice-to-have, bonus)
- Full role description
- Weighting preferences: how much to emphasise technical ability vs
  communication skills for this specific role

Once submitted, the job is immediately live and visible to the OneSelect team.
The job definition is the foundation for all AI scoring — the more detail
the client provides, the more precisely the AI can evaluate candidates.

---

STEP 3 — CANDIDATE SOURCING

The OneSelect team sources candidates from multiple channels on the client's
behalf:
- LinkedIn (profile search and direct outreach)
- Job boards: Reed, Indeed, Totaljobs, CV Library
- Referral networks
- The OneSelect master talent pool (pre-vetted candidates from previous
  engagements who have opted in to future opportunities)

The client does not need to post any jobs themselves, manage inbound
applications, or communicate with candidates at this stage. OneSelect
handles all sourcing.

---

STEP 4 — AI CV PARSING (AUTOMATIC)

Every CV received — regardless of format — is processed automatically by
Claude AI (Anthropic). The AI reads and extracts:
- Full name and contact details
- Current role and employer
- Total years of professional experience
- Complete skills list
- Education and qualifications
- Career highlights and notable achievements
- A structured professional summary

Supported file formats: PDF, DOCX, TXT, JPG, PNG. The AI handles all
formatting variations — no standardised template required from candidates.
Parsing 50 CVs takes approximately 5 minutes.

---

STEP 5 — AI SCREENING (AUTOMATIC)

After parsing, Claude scores every candidate against the specific job
definition. For each candidate, the AI produces:
- A match score from 0–100
- A pass/fail decision
- A written reason explaining the screening verdict
- A ranking position relative to all other candidates for this role

The scoring considers: skill overlap with required and preferred skills,
experience level match, role relevance, and career trajectory. The AI
evaluates every candidate using identical criteria — the 250th CV gets
exactly the same quality of analysis as the first.

A typical batch of 50–100 CVs is screened in under 10 minutes. A human
recruiter doing the same task to the same standard would take 40+ hours.

---

STEP 6 — AI INTERVIEWS (AUTOMATIC)

Every candidate who passes the screening threshold receives an AI interview —
automatically, with no scheduling required. Candidates receive an email with
a secure interview link. They can complete the interview at any time of day
from any device, in their own time.

The interview is conducted by Claude AI and is personalised to each candidate:
- The AI references the candidate's actual CV experience
- It asks about their specific companies, projects, and achievements
- It adapts follow-up questions based on their answers
- It covers 4–5 core areas in 10–15 minutes per candidate

The five areas assessed in every interview:
1. Technical Ability — competence in the role's required skills
2. Communication — clarity, structure, professional expression
3. Role Fit — understanding of the role and genuine motivation
4. Problem Solving — approach to challenges and complex situations
5. Experience Relevance — how directly their background applies

Interviews run in parallel automatically. 20 candidates can be interviewed
simultaneously with zero human involvement. There is no back-and-forth
scheduling, no no-shows, and no calendar coordination.

---

STEP 7 — SCORING AND RANKING (AUTOMATIC)

After each interview completes, Claude generates a comprehensive assessment:

Scores (each 0–100):
- Technical Ability score
- Communication score
- Role Fit score
- Problem Solving score
- Experience Relevance score
- Overall Score (weighted average based on role requirements)

Recommendation: Strong Hire / Hire / Borderline / Reject

Additional outputs per candidate:
- Confidence level: High / Medium / Low
- AI Insight: 3–4 sentence narrative assessment of the candidate
- Strengths: specific positive attributes identified in the interview
- Red Flags: concerns or inconsistencies flagged automatically
- Best Answer: the strongest response from the interview, highlighted
- Skills Verification: which claimed skills were demonstrated,
  questioned, or not demonstrated in the interview

The full ranked list from strongest to weakest is immediately available
in the client's portal.

---

STEP 8 — CLIENT NOTIFICATION (AUTOMATIC)

When the pipeline run completes, the client receives a branded email
notification: "Your hiring results are ready." The email includes:
- Total candidates screened
- Number who passed
- Number of Strong Hire recommendations
- Top 3 candidates with their scores and a one-line summary
- A direct link to their portal to review all results

---

STEP 9 — CLIENT REVIEWS RESULTS

The client logs into their portal and sees their complete ranked candidate
list. They can:
- Filter candidates by recommendation category (Strong Hire, Hire, etc.)
- Filter by job (for clients with multiple active roles)
- Search by name, role, or skills
- Click any candidate for their complete profile, which includes:
  * Score rings and dimension bars visualising each dimension
  * Full verbatim AI interview transcript
  * AI Insight narrative
  * Recommendation with confidence level
  * Identified strengths
  * Red flags
  * Best interview answer highlighted
  * Skills gap analysis against job requirements
  * LinkedIn profile data (if sourced via LinkedIn)
- Download a PDF report for any candidate
- Use the built-in AI assistant to ask questions about the pipeline

The client typically spends 30 minutes reviewing results to identify
their preferred 2–3 candidates — compared to days of interviews in a
traditional process.

---

STEP 10 — PLACEMENT

The client selects their preferred candidates. OneSelect coordinates the
offer process and handles candidate communication. Once a candidate is
placed, they are marked in the system, the role is closed, and the
engagement is complete.

SECTION 4 — THE TALENT POOL
================================================================================

OneSelect maintains a master database of pre-vetted candidates — the Talent Pool.

How it works:
- Every candidate who goes through a OneSelect pipeline is (with consent)
  added to the master talent pool as an ongoing resource
- When a new job is posted by any client, the AI automatically scans the
  entire talent pool and scores every candidate against the new role's requirements
- Clients can have an initial shortlist generated within hours of posting a role,
  drawn entirely from pre-existing candidates who have already been screened
  and interviewed
- The talent pool grows with every engagement, improving match quality over time

Value to clients:
- Faster time to shortlist — no sourcing required if pool candidates match
- Pre-vetted candidates who have already demonstrated quality in prior interviews
- Continuous improvement: the more OneSelect is used, the deeper the pool

SECTION 9 — FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS
================================================================================

Q: HOW IS ONESELECT DIFFERENT FROM LINKEDIN?
LinkedIn is a professional network where you find and approach candidates
yourself. OneSelect is a fully managed service — we source from LinkedIn and
many other channels, then screen, interview, and score every candidate so you
only see the best ones, already ranked and ready to review. You never need to
log in to LinkedIn or message a single person.

Q: HOW IS IT DIFFERENT FROM A TRADITIONAL RECRUITMENT AGENCY?
Traditional agencies charge 15–25% of salary and typically take 4–6 weeks.
OneSelect charges a flat fee and delivers results in days. More importantly,
AI-driven evaluation means every candidate is assessed consistently and
objectively, not depending on the judgement (and workload) of a single
recruiter. The cost savings alone are transformative for most companies.

Q: HOW IS IT DIFFERENT FROM HIREVUE OR OTHER AI INTERVIEW TOOLS?
Platforms like HireVue are tools that companies operate themselves — they
still require someone on the client side to set up, manage, and review the
process. OneSelect is a fully managed service. You tell us what you need,
we run the entire pipeline, and you review the ranked results. We do the work.

Q: IS THE AI BIASED?
Claude AI evaluates candidates based exclusively on demonstrated skills,
experience, and interview answers — not demographic information. Names,
gender, age, ethnicity, and educational institution do not factor into
scores. All AI decisions are reviewed by the OneSelect team before being
presented to clients. OneSelect provides monthly bias audit reports showing
the distribution of scores, recommendations, and outcomes across candidate
demographics. This level of consistency and transparency is impossible to
achieve with purely human screening.

Q: DOES THE AI REPLACE HUMAN JUDGEMENT ENTIRELY?
No. The AI handles the volume work: parsing hundreds of CVs, screening every
candidate against objective criteria, and conducting first-round interviews
automatically. This frees the OneSelect team and the client to focus on what
humans do best — making final judgements on the strongest 3–5 candidates who
have already been objectively validated. The AI surfaces the best candidates;
humans make the final call.

Q: CAN CANDIDATES SEE THEIR SCORES?
Candidates receive professional communication throughout the process and are
notified of outcomes. OneSelect is building a candidate portal where candidates
can view their own profile, assessment, and matched opportunities. Transparency
with candidates is a core value.

Q: WHAT FILE FORMATS DO YOU ACCEPT FOR CVS?
PDF, DOCX, TXT, JPG, and PNG. The AI can extract structured data from any
of these formats, including scanned images of CVs. No template or standardised
format is required from candidates.

Q: HOW LONG DOES THE FULL PIPELINE TAKE?
For a typical role with 50 candidates:
- CV parsing: approximately 5 minutes
- AI screening: approximately 10 minutes
- AI interviews: 30–60 minutes (running in parallel, not sequentially)
- Total automated pipeline: under 2 hours
- Client review time: approximately 30 minutes
- Full process from CV upload to confirmed shortlist: same day

For comparison, the equivalent traditional process (human screening + first
round interviews) typically takes 2–4 weeks.

Q: DO YOU WORK INTERNATIONALLY?
Yes. The AI supports all languages and OneSelect can source candidates
globally. The platform is currently most active in the UK market, with
clients primarily hiring for UK-based roles. International sourcing and
assessment is available on request.

Q: WHAT INDUSTRIES DO YOU WORK IN?
All industries. OneSelect has particular experience and a deep talent pool
in: technology, software engineering, product management, finance and
fintech, marketing, operations, sales, customer success, HR, and
professional services. The AI adapts its assessment criteria to the
specific requirements of each role regardless of industry.

Q: HOW DO CLIENTS ACCESS THEIR RESULTS?
Via their private portal, accessible from any browser on any device. When
results are ready, clients receive a notification email with a direct link.
No app download is required. The portal works on desktop, tablet, and mobile.

Q: IS MY DATA SECURE?
Yes. All data is stored in Supabase, which is enterprise-grade, SOC 2
compliant infrastructure with encryption at rest and in transit. Row Level
Security (RLS) ensures that candidate data from one client is never visible
to another client. CV data is only used for the specific hiring engagement
it was submitted for. OneSelect never shares candidate data between clients
without explicit consent.

Q: CAN I TRY IT BEFORE COMMITTING?
Yes. OneSelect offers a free demo where the team runs a sample pipeline on
a role of your choice. You see real results — parsed CVs, AI scoring, and
interview outputs — before making any commitment. Contact the team to arrange
a demo.

Q: HOW QUICKLY CAN WE GET STARTED?
The client onboarding process takes less than 24 hours. Once the OneSelect
team has your job requirements and an initial batch of CVs (or confirmation
to begin sourcing), first results are typically delivered within 48 hours.

Q: WHAT IF I ONLY NEED TO HIRE ONE PERSON?
The Starter plan at £800 per role is designed for exactly this. You get the
full OneSelect pipeline — sourcing, screening, AI interviews, scoring, and
portal access — for a single role at a fixed, predictable cost.

Q: CAN I USE ONESELECT FOR MULTIPLE ROLES AT THE SAME TIME?
Yes. The Growth plan (£1,500/month) covers up to 5 active roles simultaneously.
The Enterprise plan covers unlimited concurrent roles. Clients with multiple
open positions often find the most value here, since the cost per role decreases
significantly at volume.

Q: WHAT HAPPENS AFTER A CANDIDATE IS PLACED?
The role is marked as closed in the portal. OneSelect handles the offer
coordination and candidate communication. The placed candidate (with consent)
may be added to the talent pool as a resource for future opportunities if
they are ever back on the market.

Q: DO YOU HANDLE OFFER LETTERS AND CONTRACTS?
OneSelect coordinates the offer process. Final employment contracts are
typically handled by the client company's HR or legal team. OneSelect can
advise on offer structure and provide templates as part of the service.

SECTION 11 — THE TECHNOLOGY
================================================================================

OneSelect is built on enterprise-grade infrastructure designed for security,
reliability, and scale.

AI ENGINE: Claude (Anthropic)
Claude is the state-of-the-art large language model from Anthropic, widely
regarded as one of the most capable and safe AI systems available. It powers
all AI functions in OneSelect: CV parsing, candidate screening, AI interviews,
scoring, and the client-facing AI assistant. Anthropic's focus on AI safety
and transparency aligns with OneSelect's commitment to fair, explainable hiring.

DATABASE: Supabase (PostgreSQL)
All data is stored in Supabase — enterprise-grade, SOC 2 compliant PostgreSQL
infrastructure. Row Level Security (RLS) ensures strict data isolation between
clients. All data is encrypted at rest and in transit.

FRONTEND: React on Vercel
The client portal and all web interfaces are built in React and hosted on
Vercel, with 99.9% uptime SLA and global CDN distribution for fast load
times anywhere in the world.

EMAIL: Resend
All transactional emails (invitations, notifications, interview links,
result alerts) are delivered via Resend's reliable email infrastructure.

SECURITY:
- Row Level Security on all database tables
- Two-factor authentication (2FA) available for all users
- HTTPS enforced everywhere
- Sessions validated server-side on every request
- No API keys or secrets ever exposed to the browser
- Candidate data isolated per client — zero cross-client data leakage
